The Cost of Manual Operations

Estimators Locked in Spreadsheets

Your best estimators spend dozens of hours per bid on manual takeoffs, pricing lookups, and formula corrections. That's time they're not winning new work.

Abstract Sheets That Break

Excel workbooks grow brittle over time. Hidden dependencies, broken formulas, and errors that compound across projects. Every bid starts from a risky foundation.

Subcontractor Bid Leveling is Manual

You're manually emailing, calling, and tracking subs. Bid leveling takes days. Communication is scattered across email, texts, and phone calls nobody can find later.

No Historical Cost Library

Each bid builds from scratch. You're not reusing historical unit costs, labor productivity, or overhead structures from completed projects. Years of data gathering dust.

Go/No-Go Decisions Based on Gut Feel

You're making bid, no-bid decisions without clear data. Missing margin trends, cost patterns, and project fit. Leaving money on the table or chasing the wrong work.

Admin Work Drowning Payroll and AP

Invoice processing, payroll data entry, and compliance docs are still manual. Your finance team spends 20+ hours weekly on rekeying and chasing documents.
