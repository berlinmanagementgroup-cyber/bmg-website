The Problem Property Managers Face Every Day

Managing properties at scale requires juggling dozens of operational processes. Most property management companies haven't automated any of them.

Fragmented Tenant Communication

Tenant requests come in via phone, email, text, portals, even social media. No central intake. Requests slip through the cracks. You're manually triaging, forwarding, and following up.

Manual Maintenance Dispatch

When a tenant submits a maintenance request, someone has to read it, categorize it, find the right vendor, send the work order, follow up on completion. Every. Single. Request.

Lease Renewal and Rent Collection Work

Tracking 50+ lease expirations. Sending renewal notices. Chasing late rent payments. Manually logging follow-ups. This should be automated, but it isn't.

Compliance and Documentation Risk

Move-in inspections, move-out inspections, maintenance records, lease agreements. All scattered across email, spreadsheets, and filing cabinets. One audit and you're exposed.

Headcount = Growth Trap

To handle more properties, you hire more admin staff. But now your payroll grows faster than revenue. Margins shrink. You're running harder just to stay in place.

Slow Owner Reporting

Property owners need financial summaries, occupancy reports, maintenance cost analysis. Compiling that data manually takes hours every month. Late reporting delays decisions.

What We Automate for Property Managers

We've identified the high-leverage processes that, when automated, cut your admin work in half and improve service quality at the same time.

Tenant Communication Triage

AI reads incoming requests from all channels (email, portal, text), categorizes them, prioritizes urgency, and auto-routes to the right team member or vendor.

Maintenance Request Intake

Tenant submits request. AI extracts details, assesses priority, finds the right vendor, generates work order, schedules appointment, sends notifications. No manual work.

Lease Renewal Tracking

System tracks all lease expirations, sends renewal notices on schedule, logs tenant responses, escalates non-responders automatically.

Rent Collection Follow-Up

Late payment detected. AI sends automated reminder sequences, escalates to collection protocol, logs all interactions for your records.

Move-In/Move-Out Workflows

Schedule inspections, generate inspection checklists, compile photographic evidence, auto-generate reports, flag damage for processing.

Owner Reporting and Financials

Monthly property statement generated automatically. Occupancy rates, maintenance costs, income collected, vendor performance. Sent on schedule, zero manual work.
