The Back-Office Is Killing Your Growth

Four critical pain points every home service company faces, and how much they cost you.

Dispatchers Are Drowning

Whiteboards, spreadsheets, sticky notes. Missed calls during peak season. One person handling 50+ service requests. When they take a day off, everything breaks.

Invoicing Delays Cost Cash Flow

Handwritten invoices. Delayed submission. 14 to 30+ day collection cycles. You complete the work, but money doesn't hit your account for a month. That's working on their timeline, not yours.

Technicians Spend Half Their Day on Paperwork

Techs should be in the field, billable. Instead they're filling out job cards, taking photos, hunting for parts, entering notes. Hours of admin work every week that could be automated. Revenue is walking out the door.

Leads Fall Through the Cracks

Ring phone. Busy dispatcher. Call goes to voicemail. Customer calls competitor. You never know. No lead intake system. No routing logic. Just chaos and lost money.

What We Automate for Home Service Companies

Smart Dispatch & Scheduling

Automatic job assignment based on technician location, availability, and skills. Real-time optimization. No more manual scheduling. Dispatch takes minutes instead of hours.

Automated Invoicing & Collections

Invoices generate instantly after job completion. Automatic email/SMS delivery. Automatic reminders. Collect 2-3 weeks faster. Your cash flow problem becomes cash flow momentum.

Lead Intake & Routing

Every call, online inquiry, and referral gets captured. Automatic routing to the right team member. No more dropped leads. More jobs get scheduled without more staff.

Customer Communication

Automatic appointment reminders (text and email). Completion confirmations. Follow-up surveys. Upsell and repeat business alerts. Stay in touch without overhead.

Inventory & Parts Reordering

Track parts usage per job. Automatic low-stock alerts. Prevent technicians from running out of materials mid-job. Reduce emergency orders and expedite fees.

KPI Dashboards & Reporting

See what's really happening: jobs per day, average ticket value, first-time completion rate, technician utilization, revenue by service type. Real data drives real decisions.
