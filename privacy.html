Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Berlin Management Group LLC ("BMG," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website berlinmanagementgroup.ai. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site or submit a form.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide. When you submit our operations audit request form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Work email address
  • Company name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Industry
  • A description of the process you want to improve

Information collected automatically. When you visit our site, we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Pages viewed and time on site
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • General geographic location (city/region level, not precise)
  • Interactions with site elements (form views, form submissions, button clicks)

GA4 does not collect personally identifiable information. IP addresses are anonymized by default in GA4. We use this data solely to understand how visitors use our site and to improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit through our form to:

  • Review your operations audit request
  • Follow up with questions or to schedule a conversation
  • Send a one-time confirmation email acknowledging your submission

We do not use your information for marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional campaigns unless you separately opt in.

3. Form Processing

Form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form backend. Formspree receives and forwards your submission data to us via email. Formspree's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

4. Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 (Measurement ID: G-1B2L1VB82X) to collect anonymous site usage statistics. Google Analytics uses cookies to track visitor interactions. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We track the following custom events to improve the form experience: form views, form interactions, successful submissions, and clicks on our scheduling link. These events do not contain personally identifiable information.

5. Cookies

Our site uses cookies set by Google Analytics to distinguish unique visitors and track sessions. These cookies do not store personal information. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With Formspree, to process your form submission
  • With Google Analytics, for anonymous site usage statistics
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights

7. Data Retention

Form submission data is retained in our email system for as long as needed to fulfill the purpose of your request. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics default retention settings (14 months).

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at brad@berlinmanagementgroup.com.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through our site. Form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
